Father of two and Queens native, I am a runner, a taker of photos and an Engineering Manager at Business Insider.

My most important job is waking up my kids every morning and making sure they have a great day. Professionally, I am building a team and tools to help journalists tell important stories.

I love playing with my kids, taking photos of them, and running through the streets of New York City. I am married to a beautiful woman who I plan to be dating everyday for the rest of my life.

I try to make each day unique, set ambitious goals, and love the journey.

Here you'll find my thoughts on parenting, engineering, the occasional race report, and other musings. You can also check out some of my latest photography.

I love meeting interesting people and hearing their stories. Whether you're a fellow parent, engineer, runner, photographer, or just someone doing meaningful work, I'd love to connect.
